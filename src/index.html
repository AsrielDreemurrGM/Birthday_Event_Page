A Brief Story About Me

I was born on September 28, 2005. And at the age of 2, encouraged by my father, I already began exploring the technology of that time, starting by drawing in Paint and, like any child, as I grew up, I became interested in games.

Around this time of my early childhood, I faced a new problem: most games were rarely in Portuguese, a complete opposite to how most games come in diverse sets of languages right from launch nowadays. I then began studying English, dedicating myself at school right from the start, a language I continue to use and study to this day.

At the age of 10, I started my first computer course, together with my first official English one, learning the basics of the Office suite and advancing my English skills.

When I was around 17 years old and graduating from school, I discovered my passion for programming.

Since then I have been fully dedicated to studying in this field, earning my English fluency certificate in 2024 and my Full Stack Java Developer certificate in August 2025.

This journey has shaped not only my skills but also who I am today, and I look forward to what comes next.
